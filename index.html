Debbie

Managing pain can be hard. Debbie is here to help you! Available on Google Assistant and Alexa. "Hey Google, get help from Debbie"

Debiie had a successful career in IT for many years before her chronic arthritis became too much. Having struggled to find any specialist who could help, a friend came across a cognitive pain management course and paper toolset, and the idea for a pain management app was born.

Its as simple as that. You can then plan your goals, record, pain, work through exercises or set reminders.
